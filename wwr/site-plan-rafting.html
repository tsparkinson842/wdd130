Overview

Purpose

Here at Claret Hollows we on the 6th Stratum team own up to our reputation of a brutally difficult white water rafting experience. Our rental equipment prices are as unbeatable as the experience itself.

Audience

Our intended audience is for elite white water rafters who need a death defying challenge to truly feel alive. We recognize that locations similar to ours are banned to the public for saftey reasons, which is why we are looking for those who waiver away any right to sue us for death or other injuries that are likely to occur. With officials breathing down our necks, we charge a hefty premium price for a hefty premium opportunity. On your journey, you may see fellow rafters resting in peace with the chance to join them too. The way most of our guests sign up is on a desktop computer.
